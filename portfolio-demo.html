SHOW DECK PROJECT
Creative Direction

We transformed the film’s most iconic moments into short-form social content designed to feel native, shareable and instantly recognizable.

Strategy

Instead of promoting the movie in a traditional way, we amplified its most relatable details: food obsession, Sicilian aesthetic, irony and memorable dialogues. The strategy mixed editorial design with meme culture. Elegant typography and refined layouts contrasted with playful copywriting and social-first formats, helping the campaign feel both cinematic and contemporary. Each asset was designed to extend the film universe beyond the trailer, turning scenes into micro-content people would save, repost and quote naturally within their feeds.
